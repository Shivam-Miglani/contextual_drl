Try this recipe for two servings of delicious nutritious homemade mushroom soup
(If two servings are not enough for you just multiply the ingredients by two or three C or four
Make sure to clean the mushrooms first
You could use a mushroom brush or if it is not available use a piece of damp paper towel instead
Cut 4 ounces of mushrooms into slices
Chop the remaining 4 ounces
Melt 2 tablespoons of butter in a saucepan
Saute the sliced mushrooms in the pan until they are golden brown
Then remove the mushrooms from the pan and set them aside
In the same pan melt the remaining 2 tablespoons of butter and saute the chopped onions and chopped mushrooms together
Add a bit of salt
Keep frying and stirring until your onions are soft not burned
Put the sliced mushrooms back into the pan
Add the flour and cook the mixture for about 2 minutes with constant stirring
Once the mixture is smooth pour in the chicken broth carefully
Stir the mixture constantly letting the soup simmer over low heat until it thickens
Season your soup with white pepper and nutmeg
Add and pour the heavy cream into the saucepan
Heat the soup until it starts to simmer
Before it begins to boil turn off the heat
Now ladle the soup into serving bowls sit back relax and enjoy
