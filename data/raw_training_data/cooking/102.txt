The beauty of this recipe is how simple it is how beautifully the flavor of the ingredients marry together how well it pairs with so many dishes
It's perfect as an entree salad for a busy week night or right along side your favorite grilled foods
It'll be a dish that will quickly become a staple in your recipe repertoire
Cook the orzo in boiling salted water
Drain and allow to cook to room temperature
Serve immediately
If making this salad in advance store in an airtight container in the fridge without the basil or the balsamic glaze
Toss with basil and drizzle with glaze just prior to serving
