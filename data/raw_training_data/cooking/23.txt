How to make bran muffins
Preheat oven to 375 degree
Mix together the wheat bran and buttermilk
Let the mixture stand for about 10 minutes
Sift together the flour baking powder baking soda and salt in a large bowl
Beat the egg then add the oil vanilla extract and sugar in another bowl
Line each compartment of the muffin pan with paper muffin liners
If muffin liners are not available just grease each compartment of the muffin pan lightly with a little vegetable oil
Add to the bran and buttermilk mixture to the egg mixture
Then add this liquid mix to the dry ingredients in the big bowl
Mix everything only until the ingredients are blended together
Do not overmix
Fold the raisins into the batter
Spoon the batter into the muffin cups lined or unlined
Each muffin cup must be only three-fourths full
This will allow the muffins to rise but not spill over during baking
Bake the muffins for 15 to 20 minutes
Test for doneness by inserting a wooden toothpick into the center of one of the muffins
If the toothpick comes out clean the muffins are ready
Once your muffins are done remove them from the oven and let them cool for a few minutes before serving
These great-tasting muffins can be served with butter whipped cream cream cheese jam or other favorite spreads of your choice
They're perfect for breakfast or snacks and they keep very well when packed in your kid's lunchbox
