Marinade the shrimp in a mixture of the soy sauce sugar pepper corns and sesame oil
Let it sit at least 20 minutes or longer
Drain it after and save the marinating liquid
Add 2 tablespoons of corn starch and
cup of water and mix until the starch is incorporated
In a hot pan add the minced garlic ginger and chilli flakes
Add the bell peppers and the grated carrots next and let them soften
Stir in the shrimps and scallions
Let it cook for 2 minutes
Add the corn starch and soy sauce mixture
Mix it around and let the sauce thicken
When serving place some rice in the center of the plate and top it with the Szechuan shrimps
Garnish it with the minced Thai chilli pepper and cilantro leaves
